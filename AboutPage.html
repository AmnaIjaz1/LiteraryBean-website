Background:

Founded in 2015 by two lifelong friends—a barista and a librarian—The Literary Bean was
born from a shared love of storytelling and espresso. Our mission? To create a space where
pages turn as slowly as the steam rises from your cup.

Team Section:

Founders:

Team Member Bio
Alex Morgan (Founder/Barista) Alex’s obsession with single-origin beans began during their travels through Colombia’s coffee farms.
Every cup they brew is a tribute to the farmers and stories behind the beans
Jordan Lee (Founder/Curator) A former librarian, Jordan believes books and coffee are the ultimate pairing.
They personally curate our shelves with hidden gems, from vintage classics to indie authors

Timeline (Milestones):

  • 2015: Opened in a tiny corner with 10 books.
  • 2018: Featured in "100 Best Themed Cafés" by Coffee Times.
  • 2022: Launched the "Books & Beans" subscription box.
  • 2023: Expanded to include a rooftop poetry garden
  • 2024: Featured in "Top 5 Themed Cafés in the Country" by Café Culture Magazine.
